Welcome to Riyadh

Where A Traditional Past Meets A Modern Future.

Travel

Once a mudbrick waystation along desert trading routes, Riyadh is now a thriving metropolis and the capital of Saudi Arabia.

History

Riyadh is the political and administrative center of Saudi Arabia and home to the country's most important institutions.

Sources

The city is divided into 15 municipal districts managed by Riyadh Municipality headed by the mayor of Riyadh, and the Riyadh Development Authority, chaired by the Governor of Riyadh Province, Faisal bin Bandar Al Saud.
